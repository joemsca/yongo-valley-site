What we like to buy
B2B physical products, light & industrial manufacturing, distribution & wholesale, building materials & hardware, and essential services.
The edge we look for
A proprietary product, a patent, or a real competitive advantage. Something time and competitors can't easily copy.
Age & track record
Mature established business, with a clean and steady record.
Where
Based in Denver, searching to acquire in Colorado, New York, Connecticut, Rhode Island, or Illinois. Will relocate for the right fit.

A strong fit looks like

  • Repeat customers and recurring or need-based revenue
  • A proprietary or patented product with a real moat
  • Operations that could benefit from improved systems
  • An owner ready for a careful, honest handoff

Probably not a fit

  • Direct-to-consumer or Amazon-dependent brands
  • Fashion, apparel, or novelty and discretionary goods
  • Single customer or channel dependency
  • Owner-dependent without support staff
